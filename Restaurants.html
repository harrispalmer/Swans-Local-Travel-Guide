Courtnet Room - 619 Courtney St

The Courtney Room; Modern Pacific North West Cuisine. Gather at the bar for fresh-shucked oysters and craft cocktails. Unwind with friends over sharing plates in our lower brasserie. Savour a special dinner and elevated experience in the fine dining room. All of our menus feature local seafood, house dry-aged steak and local produce. Old world meets new at The Courtney Room. As an extension of The Magnolia Hotel & Spa, The Courtney Room is an elegant dining space providing genuine hospitality. Just two blocks from the inner harbour, we are thrilled to be recognized by enRoute Magazine as #10 Best New Restaurant in Victoria in 2018 and one of the Top 100 Restaurants in Canada in 2019! Whether you’re here for lunch, happy hour, a casual dinner or celebrating a special occasion, our team will ensure you will have a memorable experience!

OLO Restaurant - 509 Fisgard St

Nestled in the heart of Victoria, B.C.’s bustling Chinatown neighbourhood, OLO Restaurant pays homage to the region’s roots as a thriving trade hub. Ingredients from our farm in East Sooke are featured seasonally within our kitchen and bar, where a melting pot of techniques and flavours are used to create delicious fare and inspire memorable experiences.

Terrazza - 555 Johnson St

Enjoy fine Italian dining in an old town courtyard setting, featuring brick fireplaces and candle lit ambiance. A lively room full of industry professionals are here every night enhancing your dining experience. Our menu is inspired by Northern Italian cuisine with a pacific north west twist. We feature a diverse menu that has something for everyone. Our certified Sommeliers have created an amazing wine program to complement our offerings. Ferris Grill - 536 Yates St

Since the winter of 1991, Ferris' Downstairs Grill and Garden Patio has been a cozy space where guests know they can come to find great food. Over the years, the all-weather atrium, high ceilings, brick walls, and wooden floors have elevated Ferris' to institution status for locals and tourists who want good quality, well -priced meals in a comfortable place.
